To Earn Beans, collect Bean Codes from receipts from partnered coffee shops and coffee bean brands. After gaining 100 beans, you have earned enough to donate a coffee tree to a coffee farm of your choice. To learn more about our partnered sustainable coffee farms, visit our Farms page.

To add beans, click the add beans button to the right and follow the instructions on the pop-up window. (Pop-up blocker may stop this from happening, please check your browser settings.) After gaining 100 beans, a button will appear to let you donate a coffee tree to a partnered coffee farm of your choice.
